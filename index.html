Our Story

Where Luxury Meets the Open Sea

Azalea Cruise was born from a deep love of the Maldives and a desire to share its extraordinary beauty in the most intimate way possible. Our handcrafted itineraries take you far beyond the resort islands, into untouched atolls and coral sanctuaries few visitors ever witness.

Aboard our meticulously appointed yacht, every detail is considered — from sunrise dive expeditions and sunset dolphin cruises to private beach dinners under a canopy of stars. This is the Maldives as it was meant to be experienced.

The Vessel

The Azalea

A masterpiece of naval craftsmanship, Azalea was purpose-built for the Maldivian archipelago. Her shallow draft allows access to the most secluded lagoons, while her spacious decks and refined interiors ensure your comfort never wavers.

Accommodating up to eight guests in four elegantly appointed cabins, every inch of Azalea reflects our commitment to providing an unparalleled experience at sea.

  • Length 28 metres
  • Capacity Up to 8 guests
  • Cabins 4 en-suite cabins
  • Crew Expert crew of 5
  • Range All Maldivian atolls
  • Season Year-round
